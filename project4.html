Spaghetti carbonara

Ingredients:

  • spaghetti
  • pancetta
  • Eggs
  • parmesan cheese
  • balck pepper

Instructions

  1. cook sapghetti until al dente
  2. cook pancetta in a pan until crispy
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in abowl
  4. Combine all ingredients and serve with black pepper
